Calculations

Frequency

  • Error (ppm) = ((measured - expected) / expected) * 1,000,000

Voltage and current

  • V = I * R
  • P = V^2 / R

Reactance

  • Xc = 1 / (2 * pi * f * C)
  • Xl = 2 * pi * f * L
